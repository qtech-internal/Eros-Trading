Indenting/Importing/Exporting Services

Eros Trading Company stands as a prominent player in the international indenting business, offering a comprehensive suite of services in importing, exporting, and indenting. As a young and dynamic company, we are driven by a vision to excel in our field and to be a trusted partner in the growth and success of our valued customers and principals.

Key Features

Polymer Recycling Expertise

With over a decade of experience, Eros Trading Company has established itself as a leader in the polymer recycling industry since 2009. We pride ourselves on being the most innovative and complete closure polymer recycling company, offering cutting-edge solutions to meet the evolving needs of the market.

Global Partnerships

Our strong and close associations with partners in Japan have enabled us to carve a niche in the polymer industry in both Japan and India. These partnerships have been instrumental in our success, providing us with access to top-quality materials, technology, and market insights.

International Reach

In addition to Japan and India, Eros Trading Company has forged close ties with waste management entities in countries such as France, Germany, Portugal, Spain, and Slovakia. These strategic partnerships enhance our global footprint and facilitate seamless sourcing and distribution of recycled polymers and plastic scraps.

One-Stop Sourcing Hub

We serve as a one-stop sourcing hub for leading trading companies in India, offering a diverse range of recycled polymers and plastic scraps sourced from reputable suppliers worldwide. Our extensive network and industry expertise enable us to meet the varied needs and specifications of our customers with efficiency and reliability.

Mission

Our mission is to be an overall leader in the indenting, importing, and exporting business, delivering exceptional value to our customers, principals, and stakeholders. We are committed to identifying and providing innovative products and services that meet the evolving needs of our markets, both now and in the future. Through strategic partnerships, operational excellence, and a customer-centric approach, we aim to drive sustainable growth and create lasting value for all our stakeholders.
