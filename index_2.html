About Us

Welcome to Eros Trading Company, a pioneering force in the global plastics industry, committed to sustainability, innovation, and ethical business practices. Established in 2011, Eros Trading has swiftly risen to prominence as a trusted name in the sourcing, indenting, and exporting of high-quality plastic scraps and recycled polymers worldwide.

Driven by a relentless pursuit of excellence, Eros Trading has redefined industry standards, offering next-generation solutions that prioritise environmental responsibility without compromising on quality. Our comprehensive range of products includes a diverse array of plastic scraps and recycled polymers, meticulously sourced and processed to meet the stringent demands of our discerning clientele.

Operating from strategic hubs in Japan, Europe, and India, Eros Trading serves as a global nexus for the plastics industry, facilitating seamless transactions and fostering mutually beneficial partnerships across continents. While our operations are centred in these key regions, Eros Trading proudly embraces its role as a truly global entity, with extensive networks and associations spanning Europe, America, and Asia. Our inclusive approach welcomes buyers and sellers from diverse backgrounds and geographies, creating a vibrant and dynamic marketplace that thrives on diversity and collaboration.

  • Why Recycle?

    Absolutely, recycling plays a vital role in addressing environmental challenges associated with plastic waste. Plastics, derived from non-renewable resources, pose a significant threat to the environment due to their inability to decompose completely. By ethically recycling plastic scraps, we embark on a journey towards preserving these precious resources while promoting environmental sustainability.

  • Our Vision

    At Eros Trading Company, our vision is not just a statement; it's a commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainability. We aspire to be more than just a player in the plastics market; we aim to be pioneers in waste management, setting new standards for ethical sourcing, recycling, and customer satisfaction.

  • Our Mission

    Eros Trading Company, our mission is not just a goal; it's a commitment to action, innovation, and partnership. Over the next three years, we are dedicated to providing sustainable and cost-effective recycling solutions to the packaging, construction, and utility industries across the globe.
