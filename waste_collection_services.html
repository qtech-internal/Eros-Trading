Waste Collection Services

At Eros Trading Company, waste collection is an integral part of our comprehensive waste management solutions. We understand the importance of efficiently transferring waste from its point of origin to our treatment facilities, where we begin our transformative processes to create sustainable solutions for our clients.

Key Features

Point-to-Point Transfer

Our waste collection services encompass the seamless transfer of waste from the point of use and disposal to our designated treatment facilities. We ensure that waste is handled safely and responsibly throughout the collection process, minimising environmental impact and promoting sustainability.

Curbside Collection of Recyclable Materials

In addition to traditional waste collection, we also offer curbside collection services for recyclable materials. These materials, which may include plastics, paper, glass, and metals, are technically not waste but valuable resources that can be recycled and repurposed.

Recycling Program

Through our Raw Material program, recyclable materials collected from curbside and other sources undergo a series of cleaning, shredding, and processing steps to transform them into repro pellets. These pellets serve as raw materials for various industries, supporting the circular economy and reducing reliance on virgin materials.

Efficient Handling and Processing:

Our waste collection processes are designed for efficiency, ensuring timely and reliable pickup and transportation of waste materials to our treatment facilities. We employ state-of-the-art equipment and trained personnel to handle waste safely and efficiently, minimising disruptions and optimising resource utilisation.

Environmental Responsibility:

Eros Trading Company is committed to environmental responsibility in all aspects of our operations, including waste collection. We adhere to strict environmental standards and regulations, implementing measures to reduce emissions, conserve energy, and minimise waste generation throughout the collection process.

Benefits

  • Sustainability

    By collecting and recycling waste materials, we contribute to the conservation of natural resources and the reduction of waste sent to landfills or incinerators, thereby mitigating environmental impact and promoting sustainability.

  • Resource Recovery:

    Our waste collection services enable the recovery and repurposing of valuable resources from discarded materials, supporting the transition to a circular economy and minimising reliance on finite resources.

  • Convenience:

    Our curbside collection services offer convenience and accessibility for individuals and businesses seeking to responsibly dispose of recyclable materials, making it easier than ever to participate in sustainable waste management practices.

  • Community Engagement

    Through our waste collection programs, we engage with local communities and stakeholders to raise awareness about the importance of waste management and recycling, fostering a culture of environmental stewardship and collective action.
